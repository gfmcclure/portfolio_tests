Building a marketplace for valuable Fine Art

Fine Art Exchange

Inspired by online trading platforms for foreign exchange and other commodities, the set brief was to design a trading platform and brand for artworks with a value above £10,000. As the design developed we decided to move away from a complicated indexing model to something more closely aligned with the fine art market, where sales are unlikely to be driven by speculation. Working closely with the founder I wire-framed the initial idea, produced click-through prototypes, reviewed and then designed screens and handover documentation for the development of the live MVP.
